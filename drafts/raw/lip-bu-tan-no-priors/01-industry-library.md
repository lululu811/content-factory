## 第 2 部分：行业 / 产业链卡点库

### 2.1 AI 三大瓶颈（陈立武亲述）

**陈立武原话**："couple of bottlenecks for the AI you demand and growth. One is of course, everybody knows power constraint, some country, the power they just don't have that it get impacted. And then secondly, a lot of people didn't realize the helium impact can be also quite significant for semiconductor. And then the thirdly is everybody know right now memory is a bigger shortage and everybody tried to scramble for memory."

**出处章节**：🧠 AI浪潮下的芯片机遇与瓶颈

| # | 瓶颈 | 描述 | 关键卡点逻辑 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | **电力** | "everybody knows power constraint" | 数据中心扩产跟不上需求，morgan 篇已覆盖 |
| **2** | **氦气（helium）** | "a lot of people didn't realize the helium impact can be also quite significant for semiconductor" | 半导体加工必需：冷却剂 + 漏率检测 + EUV 光刻气体 |
| **3** | **内存（memory）** | "everybody know right now memory is a bigger shortage and everybody tried to scramble for memory" | HBM（NVIDIA Blackwell 必配）+ DRAM 普涨 + 中国扩产滞后 |

### 2.2 半导体制造关键节点（陈立武亲述）

**陈立武原话**："You need to have all the right ip so that you can support a customer. Like for example, if it is a mobile related, you've got to have low power ip set that you need to have. Without that, you cannot serve them. It's a service business. It's a trust business."

**出处章节**：💰 强化根基

| # | 关键节点 | 卡点逻辑 |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | **IP 库** | 没有对应 IP 不能服务客户 |
| **2** | **yield 良率** | 良率不行客户会撤单 |
| **3** | **defect density 缺陷密度** | 缺陷密度影响客户信任 |
| **4** | **cycle time 周期时间** | 周期时间决定客户响应速度 |
| **5** | **trust business 信任业务** | 代工是信任业务，客户需要反复验证才给订单 |

### 2.3 CPU:GPU 比例反转（陈立武亲述）

**陈立武原话**："versus one to eight in the training cpu to GPU, now I can see one to four, maybe one to one, and I'm delighted cpu become important."

**出处章节**：💰 强化根基

| 场景 | CPU:GPU 比例 | 时间窗 |
|---|---|---|
| **训练（Generative AI）** | 1:8 | 2022-2025 |
| **推理（Agentic AI）** | 1:4 → 1:1 | 2025-2026 |
| **机器人（Physical AI）** | 1:0（纯 CPU + 边缘） | 2026+ |

### 2.4 半导体工艺路线图（陈立武亲述）

**陈立武原话**："we have the you know 14a is like 1.4 nm, and we're already starting to plan for 1 nm and 0.7 nanometer is getting smaller and smaller."

**出处章节**：🔬 突破物理极限

| 节点 | 工艺 | 状态 |
|---|---|---|
| **18A** | 1.8nm | 已量产（Intel 2025） |
| **14A** | 1.4nm | 量产中（Intel 2026） |
| **10A** | 1.0nm | 规划中 |
| **7A** | 0.7nm | 规划中 |
| **物理极限** | 接近 hair 厚度 | "in a way, it's much like our hair so thin" |

**陈立武判断**："I think that path, I think we can get there, but could not be more and more expensive and more difficult to do. And that's why we need partners. We cannot just do it ourselves alone, partner with a subscript vendor, partner with equipment vendors"

**关键观点**：成本 + 难度指数级上升，必须依靠合作伙伴
